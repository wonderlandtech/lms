Attendance

Roll call that takes one minute, not ten.

Mark a class present, late, or absent from a single sheet. Every student sees their own record, so there's no more "I was here, I swear" at the end of term.

  • ✓Mark a full class in a few taps
  • ✓Students see their own attendance percentage, always
  • ✓Term reports ready whenever a parent asks
Assignments & projects

Work in, feedback out — without the inbox.

Post an assignment with a deadline and it shows up for every student, countdown included. Projects can be solo or group work, set per assignment, and grading happens right where the work was submitted.

  • ✓Deadlines enforced automatically — no late submissions slipping through
  • ✓Group or individual projects, your call per assignment
  • ✓Score and feedback land exactly where the student submitted
Recognition

A certificate a student actually earned.

When a student finishes the course, they request a certificate — you approve it once attendance and work check out. Every certificate carries a unique code, so it means something when someone checks it.

  • ✓Students request, you approve or send back with a note
  • ✓Each certificate carries a unique, verifiable code
  • ✓Printable the moment it's approved
